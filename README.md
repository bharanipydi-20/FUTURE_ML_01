# FUTURE_ML_01
 Sales & Demand Forecasting using XGBoost with lag and rolling features
 
This forecasting model predicts the future daily sales demand for each store and product category using historical sales behavior.
Instead of guessing future sales, the model identifies patterns such as:
Weekly buying cycles (weekends vs weekdays)
Seasonal demand shifts
Promotion-driven sales jumps
Holiday impact
Recent sales momentum (if sales were high last week, it will likely remain high)
The output forecast represents the expected quantity of sales, helping the business estimate how much demand will occur on upcoming dates.
This forecast acts as a data-driven estimate of future revenue and product movement, reducing uncertainty in business decision-making.
### 🏪 How Businesses Can Use This Forecast
Businesses can use the forecast to:
- Plan inventory and reduce stock shortages/overstocking
- Optimize staffing based on expected demand
- Improve cash flow planning
- Prepare for seasonal peaks and promotions
- Reduce wastage and improve supply chain decisions
